Our Story

15 Years of Excellence in Salem

Serving students, professionals, and corporate institutions with premium stationery products since 2011.

A Legacy Built on Trust, Quality & Dependability

Founded in 2011 in the heart of Salem, Tamil Nadu, Shubham Stationery has grown from a humble local store into a trusted leading supplier of high-grade stationery, office supplies, and creative materials.

Under the expert proprietorship of Nirmal Jayantilal Jain, we have committed ourselves to a simple goal: providing the absolute best writing and organizing tools to empower minds. Whether you are a student starting your school term or a purchasing director ordering bulk copier paper for a corporate firm, we treat every order with premium quality care.
